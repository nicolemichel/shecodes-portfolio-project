Coding Journey

After high school I was unsure of my career path so I took two years off to try and figure it out. As time passed I was still struggling to figure out what I wanted to do so I decided to turn to the internet for an answer - after a few Google Quiz's I decided to pursue a career in Web Development. I enrolled at triOS College in London, Ontario where I graduated with honours while working two jobs.
Since my time in college was the only experience I had in coding I was lacking confidence and concerned with school debt so I returned to work in sales where I won awards for highest appointment rate, highest validation, and rep of the quarter.
COVID lock-downs were off and on after my graduation and instead of sitting around I decided to refresh my brain and fill in the blanks with SheCodes.
