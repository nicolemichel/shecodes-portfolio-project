Coding Journey

So my journey in coding is a bit of a bumpy ride.
It all started just after high school, I was unsure of my career path and didn't want to spend a butt load of money on something I wasn't going to love, because of this I decided to take a few years off to try and figure it out. Time flew by and I still had no clue what I wanted to commit to, so, being a Gen-Z I decided to turn to the internet for an answer. After a few online quizzes I decided to pursue a career in Web Development. Shortly after this decision I enrolled at triOS College in London, Ontario where I had an 86% average (3.9 GPA), and graduated with honours while working two jobs and doing an hour long commute each way.

Since my time at triOS (including my internship) was the only experience I had in coding I was lacking confidence and concerned with school debt, because of this I returned to work in sales where I won awards for highest appointment rate, highest validation, and rep of the quarter.

COVID lock-downs were on and off after my graduation so instead of sitting around I decided to refresh my brain, expand my knowledge, and boost my coding confidence with SheCodes.
